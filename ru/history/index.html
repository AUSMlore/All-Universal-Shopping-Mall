History

The All Universal Shopping Mall (AUSM) was founded in 1995 by a group of investors who wanted to create a new kind of shopping experience. The mall was designed to be a one-stop shop for all of your needs, with a wide variety of stores and services to choose from. Since its opening, AUSM has become one of the most popular shopping destinations in the area, attracting visitors from all over the world. We are proud to offer a unique shopping experience that is unlike any other, and we look forward to welcoming you to AUSM soon!
